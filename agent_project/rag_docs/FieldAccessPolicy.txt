To: Security Staff and Event Management
From: Associate AD for Facilities
Subject: Field Storming Prevention and Post-Game Protocol

In light of recent conference-wide discussions regarding spectator safety, we are implementing stricter post-game field access controls. Immediately following the conclusion of the game, security personnel in the "Red Zone" (field level) must form a human barrier in front of the student section to discourage rushing the field. The visiting team's tunnel must be secured first to ensure their safe exit from the playing surface.

While we understand the excitement that comes with a major victory, the safety of players, coaches, and officials is paramount. Field access is strictly limited to credentialed media and staff until the visiting team has cleared the field entirely. The Public Address announcer has been instructed to read a warning statement at the 2-minute mark of the fourth quarter reminding fans that entering the field of play is a violation of conference policy and may result in penalties for the university.
