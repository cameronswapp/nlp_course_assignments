To: Volleyball and Basketball Coaching Staffs
From: Facilities Management
Subject: Scheduled Maintenance and Court Resurfacing

Please be advised that the competition court at the Wayne Estes Center will be closed for scheduled maintenance and resurfacing starting next Monday, May 15th, through Friday, May 26th. During this period, the main court area will be inaccessible to all players and staff to allow the new finish to cure properly. We are also taking this opportunity to upgrade the court lighting system to new, high-efficiency LEDs that will improve broadcast quality and reduce energy consumption.

Practices during this two-week window should be relocated to the auxiliary gyms in the HPER building or the Dee Glen Smith Spectrum, pending availability. We have coordinated with the scheduling office to ensure that strength and conditioning sessions in the weight room at the Estes Center remain unaffected. Access to locker rooms and team offices will remain open, but please use the side entrances to avoid the construction zone in the main lobby.
