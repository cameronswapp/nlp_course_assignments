To: All Student-Athletes and Coaching Staff
From: Office of Compliance & Student Services
Subject: Reminder Regarding NIL Disclosure Requirements

This is a mandatory reminder that all Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) activities must be disclosed to the Compliance Office within 48 hours of entering into an agreement. It is the responsibility of the student-athlete to upload the contract details and compensation terms to our compliance monitoring software. Failure to report these activities puts both your eligibility and the university’s standing with the NCAA at risk.

Please remember that while you are free to profit from your NIL, you are strictly prohibited from using university intellectual property—including the "Block A" logo, team uniforms, or official trademarks—without a separate licensing agreement. Additionally, no NIL deal may be structured as "pay-for-play" or contingent upon enrollment at Utah State. If you have any questions regarding a potential partnership or the reporting process, please schedule a meeting with a compliance officer immediately.
