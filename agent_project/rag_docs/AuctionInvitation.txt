To: Aggie Athletic Fund Donors
From: Development Office
Subject: Save the Date: Annual Aggie Auction

You are cordially invited to an evening of elegance and Aggie pride at the Annual Aggie Auction and Dinner, to be held on the floor of the Dee Glen Smith Spectrum on Friday, June 9th. This exclusive event is our department's premier fundraising initiative, with all proceeds going directly to the "Aggies Unlimited" scholarship fund. Join us for a silent auction featuring unique memorabilia, travel packages, and sideline experiences that money usually can't buy.

The evening will feature keynote addresses from the Athletic Director and select head coaches, offering an insider's look at the future of Utah State Athletics. Cocktail hour begins at 6:00 PM, followed by a three-course dinner featuring—of course—famous Aggie Ice Cream for dessert. Your generous support allows us to provide world-class resources for our student-athletes. Please RSVP by May 20th to secure your table.
