Subject: Aggie Student-Athletes Set New Department GPA Record

Utah State University Athletics is proud to announce that our student-athletes have achieved a record-breaking collective Grade Point Average (GPA) of 3.42 for the Fall semester. This marks the fifth consecutive semester that Aggie athletes have surpassed a 3.30 aggregate GPA, demonstrating our unwavering commitment to excellence both on the field and in the classroom. The Women’s Cross Country and Men’s Tennis teams led the department, both posting team GPAs above 3.75.

Athletic Director [Name Redacted] praised the dedication of the academic support staff and the athletes themselves. "Our primary mission is to build graduates and leaders," the Director stated. "Seeing our student-athletes thrive academically while competing for Mountain West championships is a testament to the culture of hard work we have established here in Logan." A special recognition ceremony will be held during halftime of the next men's basketball game to honor the 150+ student-athletes who achieved Dean’s List status this semester.
