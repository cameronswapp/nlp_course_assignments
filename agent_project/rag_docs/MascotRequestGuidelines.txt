To: Campus Departments and Community Partners
From: Spirit Squad Coordinator
Subject: Booking Big Blue for External Events

Big Blue is available to bring Aggie spirit to your private events, corporate gatherings, and charity functions, subject to student availability and travel restrictions. To request an appearance, please submit the official "Mascot Request Form" found on the athletics website at least three weeks prior to your event date. Please note that for events lasting longer than one hour, a private changing area and a 15-minute break away from public view are required to ensure the safety and well-being of the mascot performer.

Appearance fees are set at standard rates for university entities and external non-profits, with premium rates applied to corporate or private parties. All revenue generated from these appearances goes directly to supporting the USU Spirit Squad's travel and equipment needs. Please be aware that Big Blue is unable to speak or hold babies while in costume due to visibility constraints. We appreciate your cooperation in helping us maintain the character's integrity while spreading Aggie pride throughout the Cache Valley community.
