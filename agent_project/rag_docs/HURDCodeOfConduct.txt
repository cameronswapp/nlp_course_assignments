To: USU Student Body
From: Department of Student Involvement & Athletics Marketing
Subject: Expectations for "The HURD" Members

As members of The HURD, you are the heartbeat of the game day atmosphere at both Maverik Stadium and the Dee Glen Smith Spectrum. Your energy and passion make Utah State one of the most intimidating environments in the Mountain West Conference. However, membership in The HURD comes with the responsibility to represent the university with class. The "FanUP" initiative is in full effect this season, meaning we expect all students to cheer loud and proud for the Aggies, but to refrain from personal attacks, profanity, or derogatory language directed at opposing teams, officials, or fans.

Any student found throwing objects onto the field or court, or engaging in behavior that threatens the safety of participants or spectators, will be subject to immediate ejection and referral to the Student Conduct Office. Remember that your admission to athletic events is a privilege funded by student fees. Let’s keep the Spectrum magic alive by being the loudest, craziest, and most respectful student section in the country. We want our opponents to fear the noise, not the lack of sportsmanship.
