To: Visiting Football Operations Director
From: USU Football Operations
Subject: Welcome to Logan - Game Week Information

On behalf of Utah State University, we look forward to hosting your team at Maverik Stadium this coming Saturday. Enclosed you will find your official credential packet, parking passes for the team buses, and a map of the facility indicating the visitor locker room entrance on the southeast side of the stadium. Please be advised that Logan sits at an elevation of 4,534 feet; we recommend encouraging your hydration staff to prepare accordingly for the altitude difference.

For your post-game meal needs, we have attached a list of preferred local catering partners who are familiar with visiting team protocols. If you require ice or specific training room supplies upon arrival, please contact our head athletic trainer 24 hours in advance so we can have those materials ready in your locker room. We hope you enjoy your stay in Cache Valley and look forward to a competitive contest. Don't forget to stop by the Aggie Creamery if your travel schedule permits—it’s a local tradition we are very proud of.
