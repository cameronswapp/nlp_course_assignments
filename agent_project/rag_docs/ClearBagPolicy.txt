To: All Ticket Holders and Game Day Staff
From: Office of Event Management
Subject: Updated Security Protocols for Football Home Openers

Effective immediately for the upcoming football season at Maverik Stadium, Utah State University Athletics will be strictly enforcing the revised Clear Bag Policy at all public entry gates. To ensure a safe and efficient entry process for all Aggie fans, only clear plastic, vinyl, or PVC bags that do not exceed 12" x 6" x 12" will be permitted inside the stadium. Small clutch bags, no larger than 4.5" x 6.5", may be carried into the stadium along with one of the clear bag options. Exceptions will be made only for medically necessary items after proper inspection at Gate B, which has been designated as the primary Medical Needs entrance.

Fans are strongly encouraged to arrive at the stadium gates at least 30 minutes prior to kickoff to avoid congestion. All guests will be subject to screening via walk-through metal detectors. Please note that prohibited items—including artificial noisemakers, umbrellas, and outside food or drink (with the exception of one sealed 1-liter water bottle)—must be returned to vehicles or disposed of before reaching the security checkpoint. Our goal is to maintain the electric atmosphere of "The Merlin" while prioritizing the safety and comfort of every attendee.
